About Me

Dr. Kalpana Chauhan is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Electrical Engineering at Central University of Haryana, Mahendargarh. She has established herself as a prominent researcher in the fields of microgrids, distributed energy resources, and electrical engineering applications with over 712 citations to her work.

Her research contributions span medical image processing, industrial automation, and microgrid systems. Dr. Chauhan has co-edited the book "Distributed Energy Resources in Microgrids: Integration, Challenges and Optimization" published by Academic Press, and has received national and international recognition for her research contributions.
